# "Why Now?" Diagnosis

Every job exists because of a trigger. Identify it from the JD language.

## Common Triggers and Resume Response

| Signal in JD | Likely Trigger | Resume Emphasis |
|---|---|---|
| "Newly created role" | Company is scaling or pivoting | 0→1 builds, adaptability, ambiguity |
| "Backfilling a departing team member" | Need continuity | Reliability, fast ramp-up, similar role history |
| Heavy focus on "process," "documentation" | Team is chaotic | Organizational wins, systems you built |
| Heavy focus on "innovation," "ownership" | Team is stuck or stale | Initiative, change-making, scrappy wins |
| "Help us scale from X to Y" | Growth-stage stress | Experience with similar growth phases |
| "Rebuild," "transform," "modernize" | Legacy mess | Migration projects, modernization wins |

## Example

**JD excerpt:** *"We need a marketing lead to take our brand to the next level after a period of inconsistent messaging."*

**Diagnosis:** They have brand chaos. They want a stabilizer + elevator.

**Resume action:** Pull forward bullets about brand consistency, style guides, or messaging frameworks you've built. De-emphasize one-off campaign wins.

## How to Extract

Read Pass 3 of the JD with one question: **"Why does this role exist right now?"**

The answer is the `cultural_intent_signals` section of the extraction artifact. Label it `A` (critical) if the entire role exists to solve this problem, `B` (important) if it's one of several motivations, or `C` (context) if it's just background color.

For the candidate profile, map the "Why Now?" to the Career Direction fits:
- Scaling/pivoting → Fit 3 (0→1)
- Continuity/reliability → Fit 2 (backend at scale)
- Chaos/organization → Fit 4 (SDK/middleware/tooling)
- Stale/innovation → Fit 3 (0→1) or Fit 1 (AI/ML)
- Growth stress → Fit 2 (backend at scale)
- Legacy modernization → Fit 2 or Fit 4
